Pancakes

Pancakes for breakfasts? Pancakes with stew? Pancakes as a dessert with cream and fresh fruits?
 Pancakes whenever you fancy with this quick easy recipes.

Ingredients:

  • 300g self-raising flour
  • 1 egg
  • Upto 300ml milk
  • Butter for greasing pan.
  • 1 TBSP of sugar(for sweet pancakes)
  • 1 TSP of salt(for savoury pancakes)

Method:

  1. In a large bowl mix the flour with the salt for savoury pancakes or the sugar for sweet pancakes.
  2. Make a well in the centre of the flour mix and add the egg, slowly add the milk well whisking, I prefer a thick smooth mix so I stop adding the milk after I get the consitent I want, if you want a thick batter add all the milk.
  3. Heat a medium frying pan or crepe pan, when warm add a small nob of butter and swiss it around the pan.
  4. Carefully add a ladleful of mixture to the pan swiss the mix around to cover the pan, cover for 1-2 minutes until golden brown, then flip the mix over and cover the other side of the pancake until golden brown. Remove from the pan and place on a plate while you cook the rest of the pancakes.
